About

Peter Riesing is an active and experienced graphic designer and illustrator who received a BFA from the University of Tennessee School of Art with concentrations in Drawing and Printmaking. He recently continued his education graduating from the Digital Crafts Web Development Bootcamp to expand his software skills. Peter has designed locally and regionally for a variety of businesses including many breweries for which he has had a particular interest. He has traveled extensively to Europe (Poland, Germany, UK), China and Korea. Peter is also an accomplished and creative chef.
