Knox Brew Hub

If you know anything about Knoxville beer and breweries then you probably know Zack Roskop. Zack started Knox Brew Tours and was president of the Knoxville Area Brewers Association despite not being an owner or employee of a brewery. During the early days of the pandemic, he found a deal on a storefront in downtown Knoxville. Here he opened Knox Brew Hub, a place where he would focus on promoting Knoxville and Tennessee breweries and use as a hub for the Knox Brew Tours.
