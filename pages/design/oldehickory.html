Olde Hickory is a regional, craft brewery in Hickory North Carolina. I was brought in ahead of their 20th anniversay as the in-house graphic designer to give their brand a fresh look. My first task was to create a new brand identity. The vibe at OHB was retro and classic, people loved classic cars and the brewery has a in-house workshop for an electritian, woodworker, and mechanic. The new brand is inspired heavily by vehicle and machine badges. For the 20th anniversary I used the rings of a tree to create assets for the bottle crowns and North Carolina state illustration.

Package Design

Part of the rebrand was a complete packaging overhaul with a focus on 6 packs of 12oz bottles. The flagship brands like Ruby Lager and Table Rock Pale Ale needed to be bolder to stand out on grocery store shelves. I chose simple, geometric badges and color schemes so that each brand was easily identifiable with a focus on the beer name or an illustration relating to the beer name. I also gave each of the 6 fladships a short tagline hilighting the beer's "personality." Along with labels, 6 pack carriers and mother cartons were made working with templates and limitations for suppliers and budget. For larger format 22oz and specialty bottles I gave each brand a bold illustration sticking with the simple color schemes and retro feel I had given the overall brand.
