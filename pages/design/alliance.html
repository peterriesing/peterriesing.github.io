Alliance Brewing Company

When I started working with Alliance they already had a brand with logos, icons, and colors that had been made by a large design firm. Initially they just wanted a wordmark based on that branding and the occasional instagram tile for a new beer release. As they grew they needed to package beyond crowlers and saw that as an opportunity to update the brand. The update uses the original logos but focuses on the flame instead of the torch, a ROY (red, orange, yellow) color scheme, and bold, retro stripes. I used the stripes and bold colors to create a packaging system that is easily recognizable and flexible to fit new beers.
