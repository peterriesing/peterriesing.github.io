The Drop Inn

South Knoxville is a destination for mountain bikers with miles of trail for all levels of rider. The folks at The Drop Inn saw an opportunity to build a hub for bikers with trails, activities, and camping. I was approached to brand The Drop Inn as they prepared to start the massive build-out. They wanted the brand to be modern and clean, taking influence from some of their favorite bike and outdoor brands.
